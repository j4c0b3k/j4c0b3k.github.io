What is Off-League

Off-League is a tool allowing you to go Offline in League of Legends while being Online at the same time. Your friends don't see you active but you still play the game. Isn't it awesome? Its features doesn't end at that. It has a lot more.

ABOUT OFF-LEAGUE

Off-League allows you to appear offline in League of Legends, VALORANT and Legends of Runeterra without any loss of functionality! Talk to your friends, communicate in champion select and queue up together, all while sneakily appearing offline to all your friends.
